Growing Together

— ♥ —

When my son came into my life,
I made two quiet promises.

To learn parenting from nature.
To grow together with my child.

I didn't know then that these promises
would become a lifelong pilgrimage.

Nature slowly opened my eyes
to a love that had always been there.

And from that journey,
HuggingForest quietly began to grow—

one flower,
one story,
one song,
one season,
at a time.
