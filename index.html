Growing Together

When my son came into my life, I made two quiet promises. To learn parenting from nature. To grow together with my child. Those quiet promises became something much deeper than I could have imagined. They became a lifelong pilgrimage of remembering the love that had always been there.

I didn't know then that these promises would become a lifelong pilgrimage.

Nature quietly opened my eyes to a love that had been there all along.

And HuggingForest grew one story, one flower, one song, one season at a time.
